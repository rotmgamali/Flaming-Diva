Meet Diva

The Artist Behind Every Stitch

Flaming Diva isn't just a brand—it's a one-woman movement. Founded and operated entirely by Diva herself, every jacket you see is handcrafted with passion, precision, and an unwavering commitment to artistry.

With years of experience in leather craftsmanship and a background in fine arts, Diva brings a unique vision to each piece. No factories, no mass production—just one artist, her tools, and a burning desire to create wearable art that makes a statement.

From sourcing the finest Italian leathers to hand-painting intricate designs and meticulously sewing every seam, Diva personally oversees every step of the creation process. The result? Jackets that aren't just clothing—they're collectors' pieces.

Handcrafted

Every single jacket is made entirely by hand. Diva personally cuts, sews, and finishes each piece, ensuring the highest level of quality and attention to detail.

One of a Kind

Many designs are limited to just a few pieces—or even one. When you wear Flaming Diva, you're wearing something truly unique.

Made with Soul

There's love in every stitch. Each jacket carries a piece of Diva's artistic vision and creative energy.

"I don't make jackets. I make armor for people who refuse to blend in." — Diva
