Meet Diva

The Artist Behind The Nebula

After years of crafting customized hoodies for friends and family, Diva decided to offer her designs to the universe. No factories, no mass production—just one artist, her imagination, and a burning desire to create wearable art that brings joy and sparks a smile.

Diva collects rare patches and vintage garments from around the globe, giving them new life with apparel as her canvas. The result is a collection of clothing that serves as true collector's pieces.

Each jacket is a portal to a personal galaxy, designed not just to be worn, but to be felt. By fusing thrifted nostalgia with cosmic mysticism, Flaming Diva invites you to express your unique vibration in a world of uniformity.

Hand Crafted

Every art piece is personally curated by Diva. Her ultimate goal is simple: to bring joy, radiate positivity, and make the world smile, one jacket at a time.

One of a Kind

Every piece is truly one-of-a-kind. Sourced from vintage thrift finds to everyday attire, each garment is transformed into a unique canvas that carries Diva's distinct creative and artistic vision.

Made with Soul

There is good intention stitched into each of these original creations. Crafted with heart and purpose, our apparel is designed to uplift the spirit and connect with the soul.

"My creations are meant to evoke high vibrations and a sense of humor" — Diva
